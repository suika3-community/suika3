Code of Conduct
===============

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful
environment for everyone who participates in the Suika3 community.

Suika3 is a global project built by people from different cultures,
backgrounds, and experiences. Mutual respect and trust are essential
to sustaining this community.

---

## Our Values

We believe in:

* **Respectful collaboration**  
  Treat others with dignity, even in disagreement.

* **Good faith communication**  
  Assume positive intent and seek understanding before judgment.

* **Inclusivity without hostility**  
  Diversity of background, language, and perspective strengthens us.

* **Long-term sustainability**  
  We value calm discussion and thoughtful decisions over urgency or
  confrontation.

---

## Expected Behavior

We encourage all participants to:

* Be kind, patient, and considerate.
* Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
* Focus discussions on ideas and code, not individuals.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Discrimination or exclusion of any kind.
* Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
* Aggressive, hostile, or intimidating behavior.
* Persistent disruption of discussions or development.
* Any behavior that undermines trust within the community.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces related to Suika3,
including but not limited to:

* GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
* Discussions, chats, and community forums
* Any official or semi-official communication channels

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for upholding this Code of Conduct.

If unacceptable behavior occurs, maintainers may take appropriate
action, including warning, moderation, or removal from the community,
depending on the severity and context.

Decisions will be made with fairness, discretion, and respect for all
parties involved.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of
Conduct, please contact the project maintainers privately.

All reports will be handled confidentially and with care.

---

## Closing Words

Suika3 exists to support creativity, storytelling, and collaboration
across borders.

Let us build not only great software, but also a community where people
feel safe, respected, and inspired to create.
